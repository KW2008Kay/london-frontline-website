Welcome to London Frontline

London Frontline is a premier FiveM roleplay community dedicated to providing the most authentic and professional emergency services experience. Our community focuses on realistic police, fire, and ambulance roleplay with strict adherence to real-world procedures and protocols.

Location: London Frontline, Central London

Server Type: Professional Emergency Services Roleplay

Community Focus: Realistic policing, firefighting, and emergency medical services

üìú Community Guidelines

General Rules

  • Respect all community members and staff
  • Maintain professional behavior at all times
  • Follow roleplay etiquette and procedures
  • No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior
  • Use appropriate language and communication

Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character during roleplay scenarios
  • Follow realistic police/fire/EMS procedures
  • Use proper radio communications and codes
  • Maintain realistic response times and protocols
  • Respect the chain of command and authority

Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet connection required
  • Working microphone for voice communications
  • FiveM client properly installed and updated
  • Respect server performance and limits
  • Report technical issues to staff

üéì Training & Development

Basic Training

All new recruits must complete comprehensive basic training covering:

  • Roleplay fundamentals and etiquette
  • Server rules and community guidelines
  • Basic police procedures and protocols
  • Radio communications and codes
  • Emergency response procedures

Advanced Training

Specialized training programs for experienced members:

  • Advanced driving and pursuit tactics
  • Firearms training and safety
  • Detective work and investigations
  • Leadership and management skills
  • Specialist unit training

Continuous Development

Ongoing training and development opportunities:

  • Regular scenario-based training
  • Real-world police procedure updates
  • Community workshops and seminars
  • Mentorship programs
  • Career progression pathways
